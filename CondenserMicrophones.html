Rode NT5 - Studio-Quality Small-Diaphragm Condenser Microphone

The Rode NT5 is a high-quality small-diaphragm condenser microphone designed for instrument recording and stereo miking applications. It delivers crisp, detailed sound, making it ideal for capturing acoustic instruments, overheads, and orchestral performances.

Features: Cardioid pickup pattern, low self-noise, gold-plated diaphragm, and high SPL handling.

Ideal For: Acoustic guitars, drum overheads, pianos, and orchestral recordings.

Shure SM81 - Precision Condenser Microphone for Studio & Live Use

The Shure SM81 is a top-tier condenser microphone known for its flat frequency response and transparent sound. It is highly favored by professionals for studio recordings and live stage performances, particularly for capturing acoustic instruments.

Features: Cardioid pattern, low noise, wide frequency response, and rugged build.

Ideal For: Acoustic instruments, hi-hats, cymbals, and choirs.

Shure Beta 91 - Premium Kick Drum and Piano Microphone

The Shure Beta 91 is a boundary condenser microphone, engineered specifically for low-frequency sources like kick drums and grand pianos. It provides excellent attack, clarity, and punch in live and studio settings.

Features: Half-cardioid polar pattern, tailored low-frequency response, and integrated preamp.

Ideal For: Kick drums, grand pianos, bass-heavy instruments.

Shure Beta 87 - High-Definition Vocal Microphone

The Shure Beta 87 is a professional-grade condenser microphone, designed to capture vocals with pristine clarity. It features a supercardioid pattern, reducing background noise while enhancing vocal details.

Ideal For: Live vocals, studio vocal recordings, and high-quality speech applications.
