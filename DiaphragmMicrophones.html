AKG C414-XLS - Versatile Multi-Pattern Condenser Microphone

The AKG C414-XLS is a high-end condenser microphone known for its multiple polar patterns, exceptional clarity, and premium sound quality. Ideal for studio recordings and live performances, it captures every detail with precision.

Features: Nine selectable polar patterns, low-noise circuitry, high SPL handling, and multiple filters & pads.

Ideal For: Vocals, acoustic instruments, drum overheads, pianos, and orchestral recordings.

AKG C3000 - Professional Large-Diaphragm Condenser Microphone

The AKG C3000 is a reliable and budget-friendly large-diaphragm condenser microphone, offering warm and detailed sound. It is widely used for vocals, instruments, and studio applications.

Features: Cardioid pickup pattern, built-in bass-cut filter, and high SPL capability.

Ideal For: Vocals, guitar amps, percussion, and overhead recording.
